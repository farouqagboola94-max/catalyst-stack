# /repurpose-content

---
description: Take one long-form content piece and extract maximum platform-specific content from it. One input → LinkedIn post, Twitter/X thread, IG carousel script, and a WhatsApp broadcast message.
---

## Instructions

### Input
Paste the source content (Substack article, YouTube script, or raw notes) after the command.

### Step 1 — Extract the 5 Most Shareable Insights
List them. These become the building blocks for every format.

### Step 2 — LinkedIn Post
- Length: 150-250 words
- Structure: One-line hook → 3-5 punchy paragraphs (1-2 sentences each) → CTA
- Professional but not corporate
- Hook must be the first line, no preamble
- End with a link reference to the full piece on Substack

### Step 3 — Twitter/X Thread
- 8-12 tweets
- Tweet 1: Hook. Must make someone stop scrolling and read the thread.
- Tweets 2-10: One insight per tweet. Short. Dense. Retweet-worthy on their own.
- Final tweet: CTA + Substack link
- Number each tweet (1/12 format)

### Step 4 — IG Carousel Script
- 6-8 slides
- Slide 1: Cover — hook text only (will be designed as graphic)
- Slides 2-6: One insight per slide. Max 2 sentences each.
- Slide 7: CTA slide — follow, save, share
- Slide 8 (optional): Quote pull from the piece
- Label each slide with suggested visual direction

### Step 5 — WhatsApp Broadcast
- Length: 3-5 sentences
- Casual, personal tone — like you're messaging 200 people who know you
- Include the Substack link
- Should feel like a recommendation from a friend, not a promotion

## Output Format

```
TOP 5 INSIGHTS:
1. [insight]
2. [insight]
3. [insight]
4. [insight]
5. [insight]

---
LINKEDIN POST:
[post]

---
TWITTER/X THREAD:
1/[X] [tweet]
2/[X] [tweet]
...

---
IG CAROUSEL:
Slide 1 (Cover): [text] | Visual: [direction]
Slide 2: [text] | Visual: [direction]
...

---
WHATSAPP BROADCAST:
[message]
```
