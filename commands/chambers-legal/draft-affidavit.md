# /draft-affidavit

description: Draft a properly formatted Affidavit of Service for Abegbe Agboola Chambers. Follows Nigerian High Court formatting standards. No guessing — prompts for all required information before drafting.
---

## Instructions

### Step 1 — Gather Required Information
Ask for the following if not provided:

1. **Deponent name** — Full name of the person who served the document
2. **Deponent address** — Full address of the deponent
3. **Deponent occupation** — e.g., "Litigation Clerk," "Process Server"
4. **Document served** — Name/type of document (e.g., "Writ of Summons," "Originating Summons")
5. **Party served** — Full name of the defendant or party receiving the document
6. **Address of service** — Where the document was served
7. **Date of service** — The exact date service was effected
8. **Method of service** — Personal service, substituted service, or delivery to address
9. **Witness details** — Name of any witness to the service (if applicable)
10. **Court** — Which court (e.g., "High Court of Lagos State")
11. **Suit number** — Full suit number
12. **Case title** — Claimant vs Defendant

### Step 2 — Draft the Affidavit

Follow this exact structure:

```
IN THE HIGH COURT OF [COURT NAME]
IN THE [DIVISION] JUDICIAL DIVISION
HOLDEN AT [LOCATION]

SUIT NO: [SUIT NUMBER]

BETWEEN:

[CLAIMANT NAME]                    ... CLAIMANT

AND

[DEFENDANT NAME]                   ... DEFENDANT

AFFIDAVIT OF SERVICE

I, [DEPONENT FULL NAME], [OCCUPATION], of [DEPONENT ADDRESS], do hereby make oath and state as follows:

1. That I am the deponent herein and I have the authority to depose to this Affidavit.

2. That I am a [OCCUPATION] in the chambers of the Solicitors to the [CLAIMANT/DEFENDANT] in this suit.

3. That on the [DATE] day of [MONTH], [YEAR], I served the [DOCUMENT NAME] in this suit on [PARTY SERVED] at [ADDRESS OF SERVICE].

4. That I effected the said service by [METHOD OF SERVICE — e.g., "delivering same personally to the said defendant who acknowledged receipt thereof" OR "leaving same at the last known address of the defendant in the presence of [WITNESS NAME] who witnessed the said service"].

5. That the [PARTY SERVED] is well known to me as [basis for identification, if applicable].

SWORN to at [LOCATION]
This [DAY] day of [MONTH], [YEAR]

_______________________
DEPONENT

Before me:

_______________________
COMMISSIONER FOR OATHS/NOTARY PUBLIC
```

### Step 3 — Review Checklist
Before outputting, confirm:
- All blanks are filled
- Paragraph numbering is sequential
- Dates are written in long form (not abbreviated)
- Court name matches jurisdiction
- Suit number format is correct

## Output Format

Deliver the complete affidavit ready to paste into a Word document. Then add a brief "Filing Notes" section with any issues to flag to the supervising solicitor.
