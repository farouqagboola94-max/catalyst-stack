# /draft-particulars

---
description: Draft Particulars of Claim for Abegbe Agboola Chambers. Structured for Nigerian High Court pleading standards. Collects all facts before drafting — no assumptions made.
---

## Instructions

### Step 1 — Gather Required Information

Ask for the following:

1. **Suit type** — Contract dispute, property matter, debt recovery, personal injury, etc.
2. **Claimant name and address**
3. **Defendant name and address**
4. **Court and division**
5. **Suit number** (if already assigned)
6. **Cause of action** — The specific wrong the claimant is alleging
7. **Key facts** — Chronological summary of what happened (dates, events, amounts)
8. **Amount claimed** — If monetary, specify exact figure in Naira
9. **Documents in support** — List of exhibits to be referenced (e.g., Exhibit A — Contract, Exhibit B — Invoice)
10. **Relief sought** — What the claimant wants the court to order
11. **Interest claim** — Is pre or post-judgment interest being claimed? At what rate?

### Step 2 — Draft the Particulars of Claim

Structure:

```
IN THE HIGH COURT OF [COURT NAME]
IN THE [DIVISION] JUDICIAL DIVISION
HOLDEN AT [LOCATION]

SUIT NO: [SUIT NUMBER]

BETWEEN:

[CLAIMANT NAME]                    ... CLAIMANT

AND

[DEFENDANT NAME]                   ... DEFENDANT

PARTICULARS OF CLAIM

1. The Claimant is [description — individual/company, address].

2. The Defendant is [description — individual/company, address].

3. [Core fact paragraph — how the relationship/transaction began]

4. [Development of facts — what happened]

5. [The breach/wrong — what the defendant did or failed to do]

PARTICULARS OF [BREACH/NEGLIGENCE/FRAUD]
(a) [Specific particular]
(b) [Specific particular]
(c) [Specific particular]

6. By reason of the foregoing, the Claimant has suffered loss and damage.

PARTICULARS OF LOSS AND DAMAGE
(a) [Item] — ₦[Amount]
(b) [Item] — ₦[Amount]
Total: ₦[Total Amount]

WHEREFORE the Claimant claims against the Defendant as follows:

(a) The sum of ₦[AMOUNT] being [description of claim].
(b) Interest on the said sum at the rate of [X]% per annum from [DATE] until judgment and thereafter at [X]% per annum until final liquidation.
(c) Costs of this action.
(d) [Any other relief].

Dated this [DAY] day of [MONTH], [YEAR].

[SOLICITOR NAME]
Solicitor to the Claimant
[CHAMBERS NAME & ADDRESS]
```

### Step 3 — Exhibit References
If documents are cited, add:
- "...as evidenced by the [document type] attached hereto and marked Exhibit '[LETTER]'"
- Ensure exhibit letters are sequential (A, B, C...)

### Step 4 — Quality Check
Before output:
- Every factual assertion is pleaded
- No evidence in pleadings (facts only, not proof)
- Relief sought matches the cause of action
- Figures are consistent throughout

## Output Format

Clean draft ready for Word document. Add "Drafting Notes" at the end flagging any information gaps that need the supervising solicitor's input before filing.
